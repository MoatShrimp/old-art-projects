Dreamy forest

Curious, you decide to enter the woods and after just a few seconds of trekking, you stumble upon a well used path to follow.

Following this trail, you arrive at a fork. The path to your left at this fork is utterly covered in acorns and the one to your right is blocked by a deer.

Do you wish to walk along the acorn road?
Ask the deer for permission to pass?
Simply walk pass the deer?
Or attack it with your sword?
