Dreamy forest

The acorns are a bit round and you're having a hard time keeping your balance. You try to stay on your feet while walking along but after a few seconds you finally fall and hit a patch of the acorns hard.

But to your surprise, this in turn uncovers what looks like a face made of stone. Its eyes are closed and its mouth is ajar.

Will you try placing one of the acorns in its mouth?
Try asking it a question?
Or just pass it by?
