Dreamy forest

You walk past the deer, being wary to not anger it, but in the end nothing happens. Walking along this new path for a while, you feel calm, but then you realize that something seems to be wrong, You've been walking and walking but nothing seems to change. After a long time of wandering you feel that it's time to make a decision.

Will you leave the path to the left and go deeper into the woods?
Or will you start running instead?
